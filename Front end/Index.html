DIFFERENT TYPES OF PHOROGRAPHY
  1. Portrait Photography
  2. Photojournalism
  3. Sports Photography
  4. Still Life Photography
  • Portrait Photography: A pictorial representation of a person usually showing the face
  • Photojournalism: Photojournalism is a form of journalism which tells a news story through powerful photography.
  • Sports Photography: In digital camera terminology, sports mode is a type of scene mode photographers can use to help take better sports or action shots
  • Still Life Photography: Still life photography is a form of professional photography that depicts inanimate objects or subject matters.
